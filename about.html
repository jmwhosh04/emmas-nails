Meet the Artist

Hi, I'm Emma!

"I have been doing nails for about 4 years now and am in search of clients! I am very flexible and interested in whatever crazy designs you're looking for!"

I am a 16-year-old student at Bingham High School who has turned a deep love for nail art into a dedicated home studio. For me, nails are the ultimate form of self-expression. I've spent years practicing, refining my cuticle work, sculpting flawless extensions, and mastering detailed hand-painted art.

Whether you want a classic minimalist gel mani for school, super-strong acrylic extensions, or an absolute masterpiece of glitter, French tips, and rhinestones, I've got you covered. Let's create your dream set!
