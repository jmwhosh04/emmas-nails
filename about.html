The Story Behind Emma's Nails

Hello, I'm Emma

I am a 16-year-old student at Bingham High School, and nail styling is my absolute passion. When I'm in my room, sketching patterns and trying out crazy colors, I am in my zone.

To me, doing nails isn't just a service—it's a way to express creativity and help people feel confident. I love interacting with everyone who sits at my table. I make friends fast, love sharing stories, and want to make sure your time in my home studio is relaxed, comfortable, and fun!

Whether you have a specific idea in mind or want to try something wild and customized, I can't wait to collaborate with you on your next dream set!

Get to Know Me

Fun Facts & Studio Q&A

How did the obsession start?

I painted my nails in my bedroom whenever I was bored! For Christmas when I was 8, my brother made me a custom wooden organizer box as a gift. It held 15 bottles, and it was my favorite thing. That little organizer box is what sparked my dedication to collecting colors and painting art!

What's the craziest nail set you've painted?

I love when clients challenge me with wild ideas! My favorite project so far was hand-painting a full, detailed Winnie the Pooh set—where every single nail featured a different character from the 100 Acre Wood. If you have a crazy theme, bring it on!

What is the studio vibe like?

It's extremely chill and welcoming! My home studio is a comfortable place to hang out, chat about school, listen to music, and relax. I make friends very easily and love chatting, so you will feel right at home the second you walk in.
